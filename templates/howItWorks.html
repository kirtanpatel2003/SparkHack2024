How It Works

Follow these steps to find the perfect meeting spot:

  1. Start on our homepage where you will see few options to choose from.
  2. Look for the search bar that asks for your friend's location/address and type it in.
  3. After entering the location, the web app will display your friend's location on the map.
  4. Your current location will be pinpointed next to your friend's on the map.
  5. Finally, the app will calculate and display a midpoint between you and your friend's location.

Frequently Asked Questions

- How do I start using Let's Meet?

Just go to our homepage, and you'll see a place to enter your location. That's your starting point!

- Is Let's Meet free to use?

Yes, Let's Meet is completely free for finding the best spot to meet your friends!
